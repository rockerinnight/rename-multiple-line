How to use:

"Input" is the field to fill in the names of Test-Case to convert.
"Output" is a field containing renamed Test-Cases.
Both of the above fields support filling in multiple lines.

"Prefix" is the field to enter the string of characters that precedes the string of numbers that need to be replaced.
"Series" is the field to enter the length of the number series after change.

When clicking on the "Generate & Copy" button, the output text will be generated and copied to the clipboard automatically.
